MeetSafe Safety Information

How we protect your data and what happens when an alert is triggered.

When Does MeetSafe Trigger an Alert?

Alerts are only sent when something goes wrong — like missing a scheduled check-in, responding with a distress PIN, or going off your expected location path.

What Gets Shared?

We only share what’s necessary: your name (if set), your last confirmed location, and the reason the alert was triggered. No live tracking, no personal content.

Stealth Mode Protection

In Stealth Mode, MeetSafe runs silently and only alerts your contact if something breaks — no visible check-ins or app notifications.

SMS vs In-App Delivery

If your contact has the MeetSafe app, the alert opens directly inside. If not, we send a secure SMS with a link to this page and the alert data.

If You’ve Received an Alert

1. Try to contact the person directly — text or call them.

2. If you can’t reach them, or their response seems off, take it seriously.

3. If you're genuinely concerned, call emergency services and share their last known location.

MeetSafe exists to save time when time matters. Your action might make all the difference.

Your Privacy is Core

MeetSafe is built to only act when needed. No tracking. No overreach. Just smart, time-based safety on your terms.
