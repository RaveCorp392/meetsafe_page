What is MeetSafe?

MeetSafe is a personal safety app that works on your schedule. Whether you're meeting someone new, going for a hike, walking home at night, or just want someone to know when you'll be back — MeetSafe has your back.

Instead of live tracking, we use time-based check-ins. If you don’t check in on time, or your location doesn’t match your plan, we alert your trusted contacts discreetly.

Why We're Different

Other apps track you 24/7. We don’t. With MeetSafe, you stay in control. No constant sharing. Just smart safety when it matters most.

  • ✅ Time-based check-ins — no GPS drain
  • ✅ Smart deviation detection — alerts when you're off-plan
  • ✅ One-contact free tier — great for solo travelers & casual use
  • ✅ Optional Stealth Mode — no alerts unless there's real risk
  • ✅ PIN-protected responses — trigger help silently
