🔐 Law Enforcement Data Access – MeetSafe

MeetSafe is committed to user privacy and public safety. This page outlines how authorized law enforcement can request check-in history, emergency contact trails, or app usage data in the event of an investigation.

🧾 What We Can Provide

  • Check-in logs (times, location, responses)
  • Alert history (trigger types, timestamps, recipients)
  • Associated emergency contact(s)
  • Location deviation records

We do not provide access to message content, dating app activity, or personal media.

✅ Request Requirements

  • Formal written request or subpoena
  • Officer/agency ID and contact details
  • Case reference or relevant time window

MeetSafe reserves the right to verify requests before releasing any data.
