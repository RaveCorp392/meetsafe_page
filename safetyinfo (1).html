🛡️ MeetSafe Safety Information

Your safety matters. This page explains how MeetSafe works, what gets shared (and what doesn’t), and how alerts are triggered.

🔍 What MeetSafe Does

  • Share who you’re meeting and where
  • Get discreet check-ins during your date
  • Trigger an alert silently if something feels wrong

MeetSafe only sends information to trusted contacts if there’s a concern — like a missed check-in, alternate PIN, or manual alert.

🔕 Privacy First

  • No live tracking unless a deviation is detected
  • No messages sent unless the user chooses to alert
  • No access to your photos, messages, or matches

You stay in control. Always.

📤 What Happens If an Alert Is Triggered?

  1. We message your safety contact(s)
  2. Include your last known location and a brief note (if added)
  3. Link to this page for context and instructions

This helps your contact know what to do — whether that’s checking in with you or contacting local police.

📞 What Should You Do If You Receive an Alert?

  • Try calling or texting your friend
  • If they don’t respond, and the location looks serious or wrong, call local police

You can mention in your report: “They’re using MeetSafe — it’s a safety alert app for dating.”

📌 Questions or Concerns?

We’re available at safety@meetsafe.io for any questions or verification needs. Law enforcement may request information via secure channels if needed.
